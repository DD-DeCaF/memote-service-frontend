Hi there!

Genome-scale metabolic models are tough to understand at a glance. Memote, short for metabolic model tests, is here to change that.

For a given metabolic model, the tests in memote fulfill two purposes: They give you 1. an independent, comparable score and 2. a comprehensive overview. The tests were selected on the basis of previously established guidelines (1, 2, 3, 4) and are continuously curated by the community.

To try it out simply drag&drop your model of choice onto the field above. Memote accepts models formatted in the current exchange standard, SBML Level3 + FBC2, or a non-standard JSON format provided by cobrapy .

NOTE:
  • - Depending on the amount of reactions in your model, testing may take a while (possibly >10 minutes).
  • - Currently, the resulting report only points to potential issues yet offers only limited suggestions towards fixing these. Get involved to change this!
  • - Genome-scale metabolic models are published in two flavors: Condition-specific models vs. Uninitialized Reconstructions. Individual unscored tests may only apply to the former as they require initialization.
